The financial discount covers 12 months' worth of eligible software product core costs. It does not cover VAT.

The discount is applied against the total software product price offered by your chosen supplier exclusive of VAT. VAT will be due on the full price of the software product at point of purchase on your chosen supplier’s website.

For example, if a software product costs £1,000 excluding VAT, the VAT due at the point of purchase with a technology supplier would be £200 (20% of the total cost), rather than 20% of the discounted cost.
